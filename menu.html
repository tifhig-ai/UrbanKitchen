Brunch in Pleasant Grove

breakfast, lunch, coffee, and weekend brunch favorites

Urban Kitchen serves brunch in Pleasant Grove with a menu built for slow mornings, quick lunches, coffee dates, and weekend tables. Guests visit for French toast, Urban Benedict, guacamole toast, breakfast burritos, skillets, sandwiches, soups, salads, pastries, and espresso drinks.

Breakfast favorites

Start with The Urban, Urban Guacamole Toast, an omelette, French Toast, Strawberry Crepes, Urban Benedict, Steak and Eggs, or a Breakfast Burrito with potatoes on the side.

Skillets and savory brunch

Our Ranchero Skillet, Mexican Skillet, and Urban Skillet are warm, savory brunch plates for guests looking for eggs, potatoes, bold flavor, and a filling breakfast near American Fork and Lindon.

Lunch in Pleasant Grove

For lunch, choose brunch sandwiches served with fries, including the Pesto Chicken Sandwich, Italian Sandwich, Pulled Pork Sandwich, BLT, Sirloin Tips & Eggs, and Urban Bacon Cheese Burger.

Coffee, pastries, and drinks

Pair brunch with espresso, Americano, cappuccino, latte, mocha, drip coffee, tea, juice, lemonade, Coke products, muffins, cakes, and other sweet finishes.
